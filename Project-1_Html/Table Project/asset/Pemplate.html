Tata Punch Engine The Punch uses a 86PS/113Nm 1.2-litre petrol engine (from the Altroz), paired with 5-speed manual and 5-speed AMT options. The micro SUV is 3,827mm long, 1,742mm wide, and 1,615mm tall. It has a 2,445mm wheelbase and stands clear of the ground by 187mm. Tata Punch Variants The Tata Punch is available in four broad trims: Pure, Adventure, Accomplished, and Creative. The top-spec Creative trim gets the Kaziranga Edition.

Tata Punch Latest Updates Tata recently launched the Kaziranga Edition for the Punch. It is a tribute to the endangered Indian rhino and is available on the Creative trim level of the micro SUV. The special edition offers the micro SUV with a new paint hue, blacked-out wheels, a dual-tone black roof, and rhino motifs on the exterior and in the cabin. The interior is upholstered in a new 'Earthy Beige' fabric, and features a black-themed dashboard and unique interior trim.
